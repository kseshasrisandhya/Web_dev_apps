Work-Timings

All the branches of SBI will be working from 10am to 5pm. Any type of transactions will be done with in the given timings Every second and fourth saturdays were holidays Every sunday, all the branches of SBI will be closed.

About SBI Exams

State Bank of India provides many opportunities for young graduates as well as experienced professionals for accelerated career growth. SBI will conduct SBI clerk and SBI PO exams once in every year. SBI clerk will be conducted in the month of november where as SBI PO's will be conducted in the month of May. Salaries for SBI clerks will be 3.38LPA along with HA,TA etc.., Salaries for SBI PO's will be approximately 5LPA along with HA,TA. For both exams you will be having preliminary and mains exams. If you first qualify the preliminary exam then you will be allowed to write the mains exams and then followed by the interview. After getting success with this you be allowed to work as a member in SBI.
